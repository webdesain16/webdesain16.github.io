About Web Design

Web Design is one of many extracuriculers in SMAN 16 Surabaya, Indonesia. In Web Design, members are divided into two divisions: Programming divison and design division. This extracuriculer takes place two times a week via Microsoft teams.

About Programming Division

In this division, members are learning the basic of programming while aiming to not only master the basic, but also preparing them for competitive programming, informatics, and logics competitions. Members are also participating in an educational contest held by Web Design's Prorgamming division each month starting from two months after the new academic year.
